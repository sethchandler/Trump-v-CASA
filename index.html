A New Era for Federal Injunctive Relief

Case Overview

Main Holding

6-3 decision ending universal injunctions. Federal courts must tailor relief to specific plaintiff injuries.

Key Impact

Creates "remedial gap" for non-litigants. Shifts strategy to class actions and APA vacatur.

What's Left Open

Merits of birthright citizenship EO, APA vacatur authority, Article III constitutional limits.

Practice Pointers

Class Actions

Primary vehicle for broad relief post-CASA. Must satisfy rigorous FRCP 23 requirements.

Complete Relief

For state/organizational plaintiffs, prove "indivisible" injury requiring nationwide relief.

APA Vacatur

Challenge agency guidance as "final agency action" under Bennett v. Spear test.
